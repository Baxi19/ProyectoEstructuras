Welcome to the Domino's game!


Instructions to play this game:

1: Go to Neatbeans and Open this proyect.

2: In Neatbeans you have to open the Source Packages.

3: Right click in Libraries Folder.

4: Click on Add JAR / Folder

5: Select 3 files in the  jsonFilesLibraries

4: Run the game and enjoy it..!
